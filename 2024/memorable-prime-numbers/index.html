Memorable prime numbers

2024-09-17 Math

In case you ever have need for a large-ish prime number, here are a few that are easy to remember:

  • 314159 (⌊π⋅105⌋\lfloor \pi \cdot 10^5 \rfloor⌊π⋅105⌋)
  • 8675309 (Jenny’s number)
  • 1000000007 (a billion and seven)
  • 2147483647 (231−12^{31}-1231−1, i.e. INT_MAX)
  • 11111111111111111111111 (twenty-three ones)
  • 1000000000000066600000000000001 (Belphegor’s prime)
