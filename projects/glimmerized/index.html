Glimmerized

This page is a work in progress. Expect the contents to change as I revisit it from time to time.

For years, I’ve been obsessing over finding the perfect set of colours for this website, my coding environment, presentations, data visualizations, and even some of my crafting designs. I want a palette that

  • expresses my identity;
  • is comfortable to look at for hours on end;
  • consists of colours I love individually,
  • …but which also work harmoniously together;
  • has enough contrast to clearly convey information in data visualizations,
  • …but isn’t too heavy on warm colours;
  • offers plenty of shades, tints, and other variants to flexibly adapt to different purposes and media,
  • …but doesn’t involve so many degrees of freedom that I get overwelmed.

It’s a hard task, especially for someone with zero expertise in the art and science of colour, but at least once a year I get dragged down a rabbit hole of tweaking, testing, and comparing various colour systems. Certain elements, like the centrality of blue and pink, have been fairly consistent for a while, but I’ll likely still be making changes for years to come. This page shows the current list of colours I’m experimenting with.
