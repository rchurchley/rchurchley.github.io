Graph theory research

I earned my PhD in math from Simon Fraser University in 2017. My doctoral thesis was on odd disjoint trails and totally odd graph immersions, and my supervisor was Bojan Mohar. I also studied graph theory at the University of Victoria, where I earned an M.Sc. under Jing Huang. My undergraduate years were evenly split between Victoria and Thompson Rivers University. I am one of a bizarrely high number of graph theorists to graduate from Westsyde Secondary School in my hometown of Kamloops.
